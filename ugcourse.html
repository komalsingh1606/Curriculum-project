      About BBA

      BBA is an acronym of Bachelor of Business Administration. It is a three-year degree course that provides theoretical and practical knowledge to an aspirant in the fields of commerce, business and administration. It helps them develop their business acumen and entrepreneurial insights so that they can become effective leaders and managers in future. BBA combines business related courses and generic course. The BBA course is the gateway to numerous job opportunities in a plethora of sectors like Marketing, Education, Finance, Sales, and Government to name just a few. BBA graduates have high scope in various business industries as they offer BBA Jobs with lucrative pay since this course has a greater impact on the growth of the business industry. BBA course also enriches the leadership and entrepreneurship qualities.



      Why BBA?

      BBA course is a demand of global challenges to cop up the needs of management and entrepreneurship in a business organization. Our college is providing qualitative teaching with innovation thought based on skill development of the students.

      About BCA

      BCA stands for Bachelor in Computer Application (BCA) and it is an undergraduate 6 semester degree course within 3 years in computer applications who wish to learn and explore new things into the world of Computer languages. Today we have seen a noticeable rapid growth of IT industry in India and this increasing growth has created a lot of opportunities for the computer graduates. The BCA course is one of the most popular options to get started with a career in Information Technology. A degree in BCA is at par with a B.Tech/B.E degree in Computer Science or Information Technology. For a BCA aspirant, this degree would help in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Computer Applications. A student can end up being very successful after completing the course. Moreover, after completing BCA the students can also go for Masters in the same course which is Masters in Computer Application (MCA), many can also opt for MBA (Masters In Business Administration). The duration of the course is 3 years and divided into 6 semesters. It comprises of the subjects like database, networking, data structure, core programming languages like ‘C’ and ‘java’. This course provides a lot of opportunities to the students who are interested in computer field and wants to work in the IT sector as programmer or software developer.



      Why BCA?

      Bachleor of Computer Application(BCA) is one of the popular course among the students who wants to make their career in the Information Technology(IT) field. There is a huge scope in the field of computer and also self-employement option is available. You can do freelancing or develop your own software if you have that much skills. There are many software Multi National Companies(MNCs) which provide job to the BCA graduates. If candidate has a work experience and all the necessary required skills then he/she can hold good position in MNCs.

      ANIL KUMAR SINGH
      BCA Co-Ordinator
