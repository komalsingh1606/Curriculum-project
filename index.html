INTRODUCTION

Marwari College is situated near Tilka Manjhi Bhagalpur University on the bank of river Ganga in Bihar state of India. It was established in 1941 and the founded by Rai Bahadur Bansidhar Dhandhania. The College is accredited 'A' grade by NACC and also recognized by UGC and it is affiliated to Tilka Manjhi Bhagalpur University. Marwari College, Bhagalpur offers 31 courses across 5 streams namely Arts, Science, Commerce & Banking, IT, Management. It offers education from the Intermediate-level to the under-Graduate level in all streams viz. science, commerce and arts like B.A, B.Sc., B.Com, BCA, BBA, B.Tech and BIT, post-graduate level course in commerce only M.Com, certificate course in GST. The courses of colleges are national standard based on UGC model curriculum. Marwari College is maintaining its march of excellence way ahead of other institutions of the University with over 26 departments of all stream like Arts, Science, Commerce & Banking, IT, Management. Marwari College campus is spread over 8.1 Acres. Campus facilities such as Hostel, Bank, Canteen, Common Room, Library, Placement, WiFi and many more facility are also there.The College is a strong family of above 8000 students including technical courses and other courses of a similar nature.
